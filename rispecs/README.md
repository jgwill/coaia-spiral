## Key Principles

### Creative Orientation Over Problem-Solving
All specifications emphasize **what users want to CREATE** rather than problems to eliminate. This is the foundational difference that creates advancing patterns instead of oscillating patterns.

### Structural Tension Dynamics
The system creates natural movement toward desired outcomes through authentic tension between:
- **Current Reality**: Honest assessment of where you are now
- **Desired Outcome**: Clear vision of what you want to create
- **Natural Progression**: How structural dynamics enable advancement

### Delayed Resolution Principle
Following Robert Fritz's methodology: **"Tolerate discrepancy, tension, and delayed resolution"** - the system maintains productive tension without premature resolution.

## üéØ Key Principles Documented

### Creative Orientation (Foundation)
- **Focus**: What users want to CREATE, not eliminate
- **Validation**: Rejects problem-solving language
- **Result**: Advancing patterns vs oscillating patterns
- **Documented**: Throughout all specifications, especially llm_guidance.spec.md

### Structural Tension (Core Dynamic)
- **Definition**: Productive gap between reality and desired outcome
- **Purpose**: Drives natural creative action without willpower
- **Maintenance**: Honest assessment prevents premature resolution
- **Evolution**: Completed actions transform reality, creating new productive tension

### Delayed Resolution Principle (Fritz)
- **Principle**: "Tolerate discrepancy, tension, and delayed resolution"
- **Application**: Prevents readiness assumptions from blocking advancement
- **Validation**: System rejects "ready to begin" language
- **Impact**: Maintains conditions for creative advancement

### Telescoping (Complexity Management)
- **Mechanism**: Action steps become their own structural tension charts
- **Benefit**: Breaks complexity while maintaining vision connection
- **Cascade**: Completion flows upward, showing master-level advancement
- **Preservation**: Each level maintains creative orientation

### Context Awareness (Organization)
- **Structure**: Charts organized by life/work domains
- **Isolation**: Each context maintains independent structural tension
- **Visibility**: Cross-context patterns enable momentum recognition
- **Flexibility**: Users define contexts matching their life structure
